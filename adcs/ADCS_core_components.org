* ADCS (Active Directory Certificate Services)
overview of ADCS and its main components, explaining roles and why ADCS matters for enterprise security

- micrososft pulibc key infrastructure (PKI) to issue, validate, and manage digital certificates

- issues SSL/TLS, smartcard, and authentication certificates

- integrated tightly with Active Directory for autoenrollment

** certification authority (CA)
issues, revokes, and manages digital certificates

** certificate templates
define the properties and policies of certificates issued by the CA

** certificate authority web enrollment
optional roles for web-based certificate requests

** certificate revocation list (CRL)
a list published by the CA of certificates that are no longer valid and must no be trusted

** certification distribution point (CDP)
location where clients retrieve the certificate revocation list (CRL) to check if a certificate has been revoked
