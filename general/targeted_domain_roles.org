#+title: Targeted Domain Roles
#+Author:gabriel

* table

| group | privilege | abuse
| enterprise admins | forest-wide full control | full forest compromise (all domains)
| domain admins | domain-level full control | create accounts, access DCs, persist
| Administrators (Built-in) | local & domain admin equivalent | full control on machines where present
| schema admins | modify AD schema (forest scope) | permanent, stealthy persistence via schema edits
| group policy creator owners | create/link GPOs | deploy malicious GPOs to push persistence/settings
